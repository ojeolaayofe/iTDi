ABOUT ITcore Talent Development Initiative

Introduction. ITCORE Business Solutions Limited opened for business officially in May 2013 in Lekki, Ajah Lagos with a vision for the development of the ICT industry in Nigeria. While the aim is the development of new technology aimed for our peculiar environment, we recognized the need for a developing a workforce capable of using current technology to a level where they can redesign to fit purpose and environment. We recognized the need for improvements in training methodology for ICT products in this environment. Finally, we also recognized the need for a review of the current Nigerian workforce attitude to technology and its attendant issues. With this in mind we decided to tie up as a franchise partner with an international ICT training organization with pedigree and wherewithal to help us carry out our main goal – developing the Nigerian workforce for tomorrow’s technology. After extensive research we picked NIIT, the largest ICT training company in Asia and Africa and a company listed among the top 20 ICT firms in the world. NIIT Nigeria as a whole has trained over 200,000 students in the last 19 years. Our NIIT@Skool division has grown in leaps and bounds, signing up with a number of schools with a view to developing the future workforce. Our ITCORE Talent Development Initiative (iTDi) is a non-for-profit organisation formed to cater for the need of unreached, uncared, unattended and underprivileged individuals to ensure inclusive talent development in Nigeria. This initiative is to promote outstanding Information Technology skills to address the need of the underprivileged ones of the country through inclusive ICT talent development, in a way that is scalable and sustainable.

Our Objectives Includes

• To promote outstanding ICT skills that addresses the need of the underserved, disadvantaged and underprivileged Nigerians. • To provide ICT employability and entrepreneurship skills trainings to transform unemployed youths to a unique breed of 211st century workforce.
• To provide an environment for vigorous but civil, intelligent, good-faith, and respectful discourse and advocacy on ways to improve the education and employability of youths at the grassroots and draw out a sustainable training and skill acquisition interventions model to address them with the aim of evolving innovative and sustainable solutions.
• To provide talent discovery through various competitions and exhibitions in rural and urban areas of Nigeria.
• To conduct research that contributes to the production of high-quality scholarship, better mentally equipped individuals, and a more robust discourse about the structures and value of ICT employability sensitization that shape life goals in both the public and private sectors.
• To promote and collaborate with academic national and international bodies to create effective intervention program that is geared towards discovering talent in individual, upgrading the skills base and employability of teeming youths while focusing on entrepreneurship skills of underprivileged ones in rural and urban areas.
• To promote and support individuals in different areas of the economy who are talented and underprivileged in all sectors like art, culture, sports, science and all areas where talents can be found and developed.

Our Organisation Structure

ITCORE Talent Development Initiative has a pool of staff with diverse background and experience. The organization is headed by an executive director that provides strategic leadership towards achieving the organization's mission and objectives. The executive director report directly to board of directors. The finance and admin director, ICT and training director, and marketing and communications director all report to the executive director. They all have direct reporting relationship with the finance and admin officer, performance officer, ICT officer, faculty and training officers, career counselors, and public relations officer
