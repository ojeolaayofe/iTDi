Our Main Objectives

At ITDI our major objetcives are

We Train to be the Best

• To provide ICT employability and entrepreneurship skills trainings to transform unemployed youths to a unique breed of 211st century workforce.

• To provide talent discovery through various competitions and exhibitions in rural and urban areas of Nigeria.

Give Inspiration

• To provide an environment for vigorous but civil, intelligent, good-faith, and respectful discourse and advocacy on ways to improve the education and employability of youths at the grassroots and draw out a sustainable training and skill acquisition interventions model to address them with the aim of evolving innovative and sustainable solutions.

Growth of IT Ecosystem

• To conduct research that contributes to the production of high-quality scholarship, better mentally equipped individuals, and a more robust discourse about the structures and value of ICT employability sensitization that shape life goals in both the public and private sectors..

Our Past Projects

These are few of our past Project

Education for every child

• In 2014, we partnered with Amuwo Odofin Local Government in Lagos to train 200+ youths in ICT skills development, with major focus on: Hardware and Networking, Multimedia, Management Information System and Microsoft office amongst others.

Unique Training in ICT

• In 2016-2017, we partnered CCTL Youth Empowerment Initiative (CYEI) in Lagos to provide unique trainings in ICT employability and entrepreneurship skills to 660 unemployed graduates yearly.

Empowering the People

• In 2017, we partnered with 10 community leaders (Traditional Rulers) to provide ICT trainings on Microsoft Office to 100 unemployed and underserved youths in Ogun State.
